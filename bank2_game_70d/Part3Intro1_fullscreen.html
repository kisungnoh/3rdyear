In this part, you will play {{ part3_rounds }} more games and make the same decisions as before, with three additional tasks. Below describes the first two tasks, which you will see in Period 1.

On the same screen where you choose to withdraw or stay in Period 1, you will also report your belief about

  • What the other participant will choose in Period 1.
  • The state of your bank.

On the same screen where you choose to withdraw or stay in Period 2, you will also report your belief about

  • what the other participant will choose in Period 2.

You will indicate your belief using sliders. Below is a preview of how the sliders appear on the actual screen.

Belief about what the other participant will choose in Period 1.

You will report your belief by moving the upper slider. The percentage on the left indicates how likely you think it is that the other participant will choose to withdraw, while the percentage on the right indicates how likely you think it is that the other participant will choose to stay.


Belief about the state of your bank.

You will report your belief by moving the lower slider. You will click twice to set both knobs, then dragging either knob to adjust your belief. The percentage on the left indicates how likely you think it is that your bank is in the Low state, the percentage in the middle indicates how likely you think it is that your bank is in the Medium state, and the percentage on the right indicates how likely you think it is that your bank is in the High state.

Once all three values are displayed, you can freely move either knob to adjust your beliefs for each state.
