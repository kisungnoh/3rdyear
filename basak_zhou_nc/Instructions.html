Welcome to this experiment. You will play {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds and will be randomly matched with a different partner each round.

In each round, you and your partner each have {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} points deposited in a bank.

Bank Fundamental

Each round, the bank has a fundamental state (Bad, Medium, or Good) that determines the payoffs. You will not know the bank fundamental until the end of the round.

Two Decision Periods

Period 1

Both players simultaneously decide: Withdraw or Stay

If you withdraw, you get {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} points and cannot make further decisions this round.

Period 2

If you stayed in Period 1, you will see what the other player chose, then decide again: Withdraw or Stay

Withdrawing in Period 2 costs {{ C.DELAY_COST }} points.

Payment

At the end of the experiment, one round will be randomly selected and you will be paid based on your earnings in that round.
