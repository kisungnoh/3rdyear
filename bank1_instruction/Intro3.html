Earnings

After both depositors make their decisions, you will learn how many points you earned and what action the other depositor chose. This concludes the match.

This part consists of 10 matches. At the end of the experiment, one match will be randomly selected for payment, and your earnings from that match will be converted to dollars at the rate of 1 point = {{C.REAL_WORLD_CURRENCY_PER_POINT}} dollar.


Summary
  1. You will start a match with a randomly paired participant, who is likely to change from match to match.
  2. The bank state is randomly determined and revealed at the start of each match:
    • Medium state with 70% probability
    • Low or High state with 15% probability each
  3. You make decisions across two stages.
    • If you choose action A in Stage 1, no further decision is needed.
    • If you choose action B in Stage 1, you proceed to Stage 2 and choose again between action A and action B.
  4. You do not know in Stage 2 whether the other depositor chose action A or action B in Stage 1.
  5. At the end of each match, you will be told how many points you received and the other depositor's action.
  6. At the end of the experiment, one match is randomly selected for payment (1 point = {{C.REAL_WORLD_CURRENCY_PER_POINT}} dollar).
