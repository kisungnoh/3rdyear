Decisions

You will make your decision over two periods.

In Period 1, both you and the other participant in the bank simultaneously choose whether to withdraw or stay.

  • If you choose to withdraw, you take 10 points out and make no further decisions.
  • If you choose to stay, you move to Period 2.

In Period 2, only those who chose to stay in Period 1 will again choose whether to withdraw or stay.

After both you and the other have made decisions, you will see how many points you received, along with the other's decision.

On the next three pages, you will see the game screen and payoff information for each state (Low, Medium, and High).
