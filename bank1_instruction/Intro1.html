Overview

In this part, you will be randomly paired with another participant and play 10 games. In each game, both you and the other participant will act as customers in the same bank.

  • You start each game with 10 points in your deposit account.
  • The bank can be in one of three states: Low, Medium, and High.
  • You will decide whether to withdraw your money or stay in the bank.

Your earnings each game will depend on the bank's state, your decision, and the decision of the other participant you are paired with.
