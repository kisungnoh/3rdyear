Earnings

After both you and the other depositor make decisions, you will learn how many points you received along with the state of your bank, the state of the Pre-Bank, and what action the other depositor chose. This concludes the game.

This part consists of 10 games. At the end of the experiment, one game will be randomly selected for payment, and your earnings from that game will be converted to dollars at the rate of 1 point = {{C.REAL_WORLD_CURRENCY_PER_POINT}} dollar.


Summary

Here is how today's experiment proceeds:

  1. You are randomly paired with another participant. The pair member is likely to change each game.
  2. The state of your bank and the Pre-Bank are unknown, and all three states are possible. However, your bank is in the same state as the Pre-Bank with {{C.STATE_CORR}}% probability, and in one of the other two states with {{unmatched_corr}}% probability each.
  3. In Stage 1, you see the A-count (the number of Pre-Bank depositors who chose A or BA), then choose between action A and action B.
  4. If you choose action A in Stage 1, you don't have any more decision to make.
  5. If you choose action B in Stage 1, you move to Stage 2, where you receive a message from the Observer and choose between action A and B again. You do not know whether the other depositor has also reached Stage 2 or not.
  6. At the end of the game, you will be told your points, the state of your bank, the state of the Pre-Bank, and the other depositor's action.
  7. This part consists of 10 games. At the end of the experiment, one game will be randomly selected for payment, and your earnings from that game will be converted to dollars at the rate of 1 point = {{C.REAL_WORLD_CURRENCY_PER_POINT}} dollar.
