Earnings

After both you and the other depositor make decisions, you will learn how many points you earned along with the state of your bank, the state of the Pre-Bank, and what action the other depositor chose. This concludes the match.

This part consists of 10 matches. At the end of the experiment, one match will be randomly selected for payment, and your earnings from that match will be converted to dollars at the rate of 1 point = {{C.REAL_WORLD_CURRENCY_PER_POINT}} dollar.


Summary
With the new features added, the following describes how a match proceeds in today's experiment.
  1. You will start a match with a randomly paired participant, who is likely to change from match to match.
  2. The state of your bank is unknown, and all three states are possible. However,
    • With 70% probability, your bank is likely to be in the same state as the bank selected from the previous experiment (Pre-Bank).
    • With 15% probability, your bank is likely to be in one of the other two states.
  3. In Stage 1, you will receive information about how many depositors in the Pre-Bank chose action A in either Stage 1 or 2 (the A-count).
  4. If you choose action A in Stage 1, you don't have any more decision to make.
  5. If you choose action B in Stage 1, you will move to Stage 2.
  6. In Stage 2, you will receive a message from the Observer, and then choose between action A and B again.
    • You don't see the other depositor's action during decision-making. That is, when you are in Stage 2, you don't know whether the other depositor is also in Stage 2 and is making the second decision.
  7. At the end of the match, you will be told how many points you received, along with the state of your bank, the state of the Pre-Bank, and the other depositor's action.
