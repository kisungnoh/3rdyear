Part 2 describes today's experiment. Similar to the previous experiment described in Part 1, you will be randomly paired with another participant and make decisions as a customer in a bank for 10 games.

  • You will start with 10 points in your deposit account.
  • The bank can be in one of three states: Low, Medium, and High.
  • You will decide whether to withdraw your money or stay in the bank.
    • In Period 1, both you and the participant you are paired with simultaneously chose either to withdraw or to stay.
    • Those who choose to stay in Period 1 moves to Period 2, where they choose again to withdraw or stay.

You may receive different earnings depending on your decision, the decision of the participant you are paired with, and the bank's state. Importantly, the bank's state will be determined based on the previous experiment described in Part 1.


On the following pages, you will learn how today's experiment differs from the previous one.

Note: The Part 2 instructions will remain available during the game in summarized form, so you can refer to them whenever needed.
