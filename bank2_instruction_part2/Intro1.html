Today's experiment is similar to the previous experiment. You will act as a depositor in a bank and make decisions for 10 games.

The basic structure is the same as in Part 1:

  • You are randomly paired with another participant in this room each game. The pair member is likely to change game to game. You and the pair member are the only depositors in your bank.
  • You will start each game with 10 points in your deposit account and choose between action A and action B over two stages.
  • Payoffs depend on the bank state and both depositors' actions

The payoff tables are unchanged from Part 1. Each cell shows (your payoff, the other's payoff).
