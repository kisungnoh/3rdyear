Earnings

After both you and the other participant make decisions, you will learn how many points you received along with the state of your bank, the state of the Pre-Bank, and what action the other participant chose. This concludes the game.

This part consists of 10 games. At the end of the experiment, one game will be randomly selected for payment, and your earnings from that game will be converted to dollars at the rate of 1 point = {{C.REAL_WORLD_CURRENCY_PER_POINT}} dollar.


Pairings

At the start of each game, you will be randomly paired with another participant. That means you are likely to play with a different participant in each game.
