The bank states are unknown in today's experiment, but you will receive two additional pieces of information which may help you make your decisions.


Pre-Bank Actions

Recall that in the previous experiment, each bank had two depositors who made decisions in Stage 1 and possibly Stage 2, as shown in the figure below.

Stage 1 Stage 2 (labels)
Depositor:
• action A : A
• action B
• action A : BA
• action B : BB

Before making your decisions today, you will be told how many depositors in the Pre-Bank chose action A in either stage (A or BA, i.e., not BB). We will call this number A-count because it counts the number of depositors who ended up choosing action A. Since there were two depositors in the Pre-Bank, the A-count can be 0, 1, or 2.

This may help you guess the Pre-Bank's state. And since your bank's state depends on the Pre-Bank's state, it may also help you guess the likely state of your bank.
