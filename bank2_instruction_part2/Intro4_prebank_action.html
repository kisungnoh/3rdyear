Pre-Bank Withdrawals

In Period 1, you will be told how many of the two participants in the Pre-Bank chose to withdraw in either Period 1 (W) or Period 2 (SW). These Pre-Bank Withdrawals can take a value of 0, 1, or 2.

  • W: Withdraw (Period 1)
  • SW: Stay (Period 1) → Withdraw (Period 2)
  • SS: Stay (Period 1) → Stay (Period 2)

This may help you guess the Pre-Bank's state. Since your bank's state depends on the Pre-Bank's state, it may also help you infer the likely state of your bank.

Given the table and the Pre-Bank withdrawals, you will make your choice in Period 1. If you choose to stay, then you will move to the next page for Period 2. The other participant may also move to Period 2 or not, depending on their choice in Period 1.
