Bank State Is Unknown

In the previous experiment (Part 1), participants knew the bank's state when making decisions.

In today's experiment, however, you will not be told the state of your bank. On the game screen, you will see all three payoff tables for the Low, Medium, and High states. You will need to make decisions (withdraw vs. stay) without knowing whether your bank is in a Low, Medium, or High state.

Instead, your bank's state will be determined according to the rule described on the next page.
