Summary

Here is how today's experiment proceeds:

  1. You are randomly paired with another participant. The pair is likely to change from game to game.
  2. The state of your bank and the Pre-Bank are unknown, and all three states are possible. However, your bank is in the same state as the Pre-Bank with {{C.STATE_CORR}}% probability, and in one of the other two states with {{unmatched_corr}}% probability each.
  3. In Period 1, you see the number of Pre-Bank withdrawals (W or SW), then choose whether to withdraw or stay.
  4. If you choose to withdraw in Period 1, you don't have any more decision to make.
  5. If you choose to stay in Period 1, you move to Period 2, where you receive the Observer's message and choose to withdraw or stay again.
  6. At the end of the game, you will be told your points, the state of your bank, the state of the Pre-Bank, and the other participant's choice.
  7. This part consists of 10 games. At the end of the experiment, one game will be randomly selected for payment, and your earnings from that game will be converted to dollars at the rate of 1 point = {{C.REAL_WORLD_CURRENCY_PER_POINT}} dollar.
