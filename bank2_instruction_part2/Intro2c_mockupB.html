Along with the A-count, below describes the second piece of information which may help you make your decisions.


Observer's Message

If you choose action B in Stage 1 and move to Stage 2, a computerized agent called the Observer will send you a message — "Left" or "Right" — before you make your Stage 2 decision. The message depends on your bank's state and the other depositor's Stage 1 action:

Your bank's state Other depositor's Stage 1 action Message you receive
Low any "Left"
Medium Action A "Left"
Medium Action B "Right"
High any "Right"

The message does not reveal which specific case triggered it. However, the Observer always tells the truth,, so you can rely on the message.

The above table will be shown to you when you make decisions. You do not need to memorize the case.

Note: You will NOT receive the message if you chose action A in Stage 1.
