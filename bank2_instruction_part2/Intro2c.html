A: action A (Stage 1) BA: action B (Stage 1) → action A (Stage 2) BB: action B (Stage 1) → action B (Stage 2)

If you choose action B in Stage 1, you will receive a message from the Observer (a computerized agent) when you make your Stage 2 decision. The message will be either "Left" or "Right":

  • "Left" if:
    • the state of your bank is Low, or
    • the state of your bank is Medium, and the other depositor chose action A in Stage 1.
  • "Right" if:
    • the state of your bank is High, or
    • the state of your bank is Medium, and the other depositor chose action B in Stage 1.

The message does not reveal which specific case triggered it, but the Observer always reports truthfully.

Note: The message is about your bank's state, not the Pre-Bank's. You will only receive it if you chose action B in Stage 1.
