Observer's Message

If you choose to stay in Period 1 and move to Period 2, a computerized agent called the Observer will send you a message — "Left" or "Right" — before you make your Period 2 decision. The message depends on the actual state of your bank and the other participant's Period 1 choice: ▶ hover a row to highlight the relevant cells above

Your bank's state Other's Period 1 choice Message you receive
Low any "Left"
Medium Withdraw "Left"
Medium Stay "Right"
High any "Right"

The message does not reveal which specific case triggered it. However, the Observer always tells the truth, so you can rely on the message.

NOTE: You will NOT receive the message if you chose to withdraw in Period 1.
