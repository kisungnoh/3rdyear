Decisions and Payoffs

Your bank will be in one of three states: Low, Medium, or High.
Your payoffs depend on the bank state, your own action, and the other's action.

The table below shows the payoffs for each combination. Each cell shows (your payoff, other's payoff).
You will be provided the same tables when you make decisions.

As the state moves from Low to Medium to High, higher payoffs become possible:
▶ hover a line to highlight the state

  • In the Low state, you may receive between 0 and 10.
  • In the Medium state, you may receive between 0 and 18.
  • In the High state, you may receive between 10 and 18.

In each match, the state is randomly determined with the following probabilities: Low (15%), Medium (70%), High (15%).

You will know the exact state when you make your decision, and the corresponding payoff table will be presented.

To proceed, click here, or the second tab on the left.

Below describes how your action affects your payoffs.
▶ hover a line to highlight the table row

  • If you choose action A in Stage 1:
    • You will receive 10 points (your deposit), regardless of the bank state and the other's action.
  • If you choose action B in Stage 1:
    • If you then choose action A in Stage 2 (labeled BA): your payoff will be either 9 or 10, depending on the bank state and the other's action.
    • If you then choose action B in Stage 2 (labeled BB): your payoff will be either 0 or 18, depending on the bank state and the other's action.

The following details how the payoffs from choosing action B in Stage 1 (leading to BA or BB) can vary.

  • In the Low and High states, your payoff does not depend on the other's action:
    • BA always earns 9 in Low and 10 in High.
    • BB always earns 0 in Low and 18 in High.
  • In contrast, in the Medium state, your payoff depends on the other's action:
    • BA earns 10 if the other depositor chooses BB;
      otherwise, BA earns 9.
    • BB earns 18 if the other depositor also chooses BB;
      otherwise, BB earns 0.
