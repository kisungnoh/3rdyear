Decisions

Participants made their decisions over two periods.

In Period 1, both participants in a pair simultaneously chose whether to withdraw or stay.

  • If a participant chose to withdraw, they took 10 points out and made no further decisions.
  • If a participant chose to stay, they moved to Period 2.

In Period 2, only those who chose to stay in Period 1 again chose whether to withdraw or stay.

After both participants made their decisions, they saw how many points they received, along with the other's decision.

On the next pages, you will see the sample game screen and payoff information for each state (Low, Medium, and High).
