In today's experiment, you will be randomly paired with another participant and make decisions as a customer in a bank.

  • You will start with 10 points in your deposit account.
  • The bank can be in one of three states: Low, Medium, and High.
  • You will decide whether to withdraw your money or stay in the bank.

You may receive different earnings depending on your decision, the decision of the participant you are paired with, and the bank's state. Importantly, the bank's state will be determined based on a similar experiment that other people recently participated in.

In Part 1, you will learn about that previous experiment. Afterward, you will receive instructions for today's game. Understanding the previous experiment will help you make better decisions and earn more today.
