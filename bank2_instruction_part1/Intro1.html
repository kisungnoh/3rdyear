Overview

In a previous experiment, participants were randomly paired with one another and played 40 games. In each game, both participants in a pair acted as customers in the same bank.

  • Each participant started each game with 10 points in their deposit account.
  • The bank could be in one of three states: Low, Medium, and High.
  • Each participant decided whether to withdraw their money or stay in the bank.

Participants received different earnings depending on their decision, the decision of the participant they were paired with, and the bank's state.
